About Roy's Apparels

Affordable and Quality Textile Since 1992

Roys Apparels Pvt Ltd started with a dream in 1992 by our founder Mr Nanda Dulal Roy with three workers and who dreamt of creating a world class garment company which would be grounded on the principle of excellence. Since then the company has grown to touch new levels of excellence with 500 direct Employee and 3000 indirect workers till date.

Fabrics, Garments and other knitted products are manufactured and shipped to different locations across India every year.
